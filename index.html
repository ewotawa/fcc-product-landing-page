Our Story

We founded Jack's Lanterns in 2006 to provide premium, artisan-carved pumpkins to our community in Oakville, Mo. Over the years, our reach has grown to the St. Louis Metro area, and we now not only offer our carved pumpkins but also carve other fruits and vegetables and will style your display on-site.

Jack's Lanterns draws its designs from a variety of sources. We can provide a Victorian greeting card tableau, a family-friendly haunted hayloft, and a variety of other arrangements.
